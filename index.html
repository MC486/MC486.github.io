Welcome to My AI Word Game Portfolio

This portfolio showcases the design, evolution, and enhancement of my Python-based AI Word Strategy Game, developed as the capstone project for my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Southern New Hampshire University. The project builds on my prior Q-learning AI maze solver and evolves it into a modular, extensible word game framework with future AI intelligence and persistent gameplay tracking. This site presents the game, its enhancements, and the technical foundations that power it.
